I use Mission Control in macOS regularly while managing multiple applications. A common challenge arises with numerous apps running simultaneouslyâ€”it becomes difficult to distinguish between similar applications visually. Apple previously displayed app icons on window representations, something many users wanted restored.

Here's a tip: **You can use quick view while in Mission Control. When the tiny window is highlighted, tap the space bar to make it bigger.**

This quick view feature allows you to enlarge a window preview for better identification of applications when navigating Mission Control's interface.
