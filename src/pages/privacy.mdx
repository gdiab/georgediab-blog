## Privacy Policy

This page describes the privacy practices for apps published by George Diab.

### Arbatash

Arbatash is a premium card game and does not use advertising or third-party tracking.

The app is designed to work without account creation. Gameplay state, preferences, and settings may be stored locally on your device so the app can function properly.

Arbatash does not sell personal data.

If Arbatash's data practices change in the future, this privacy policy will be updated to reflect those changes.

### AnxiLog

AnxiLog stores user-entered information needed to provide the journaling, mood tracking, and subscription experience inside the app.

Subscription status and purchase events may be processed through platform billing providers and RevenueCat in order to manage access to paid features.

AnxiLog does not sell personal data.

If AnxiLog's data practices change in the future, this privacy policy will be updated to reflect those changes.
